The IB Decision (11th–12th Grade)

RECOMMENDED HL COMBO

  • Computer Science — Core skill, opens all tech doors
  • Math AA — Keeps STEM paths alive
  • Drama — Creative outlet, soft skills, burnout protection

WHY THIS WORKS

CS + Math = technical foundation. Drama = communication, collaboration, creative thinking. The combo serves game design, product leadership, and hybrid creative-tech roles that don't have names yet.

Physics note: Keep at SL. Preserves the space curiosity without the grind. Space industry needs more than physicists.

Netherlands Deep Dive

Netherlands offers excellent English-taught bachelor's programs in CS and game design, ideal for your IB profile (CS/Math/Drama HLs). They're affordable for EU students (~€2,300/year statutory fee), with strong industry networks.

DECISION FRAMEWORK

THE FORK IN THE ROAD

CS degree (UvA, TU/e, Radboud) → Learn to code deeply, games become one application of skills

Game degree (BUas, HKU, Hanze) → Learn to make games specifically, with varying depth

At 18, with "curious about CS" + "likes games" but not certain about games as career:
• Safer bet: CS at UvA or TU Eindhoven → can still enter game industry later
• All-in on games: BUas Breda → best in class, but specialized path
